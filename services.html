Scholarship Program

The program provides monitory support for tuition fees, books, stationery, examination fee and transport and/or hostel expenses. CARE awards 1000 scholarships annually. This program has been a life-changing initiative for countless families whose children after graduating from Pakistan’s best college and universities, have gone on to become doctors, engineers, bankers and so on.



Qurbani/Sadaqah Program

The funds raised through these services are spent on orphans & needy children. If anyone wants to carry out the qurbani at home, animals for the purpose are provided at very reasonable rates otherwise slaughter services at Charity. can always be availed. In 2017, Charity. distributed over 120kg meat among needy families who don’t come out to beg on the roads. With each year, we hope our capacity to give increases & that is not possible without the unwavering support of our donors.



Zakat Program

We get most of our funds during the Holy Month of Ramzan, thanks to the generous spirit of the Muslims who make sure that the less privileged are not forgotten on this special occasion. This zakat helps us not only to provide shelter, food & assistance program which aims to help those in need of critical funds for one reason or the other. In Ramadan, Charity. also distributes food packages to poor families & holds collective iftaris on behalf of the sponsors at different locations as the sponsor desires.



Clean Environment Program

Our Clean Environment Program embodies our dedication to a healthier planet, focusing on reducing pollution, encouraging recycling, and fostering sustainable living. Through clean-up campaigns, educational initiatives, and partnerships, we tackle environmental challenges head-on. This program is designed to empower individuals and communities to become active environmental stewards, promoting a greener, more sustainable future for all.
